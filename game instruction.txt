How to play this game

1. Enter your name and set the game duration (in seconds).
2. (Optional) Set the number of space objects to collect.
3. Click Play to start the game.
4 Use arrow keys or WASD to move the Phyton around the board.
5.  Pause/Resume: Press `P` or click Pause button
5. Collect  all space objects (small = 5 points, large = 20 points).
6. Avoid the enemy spaceship chasing you.
